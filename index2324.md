S. Sentance, E. Barendsen, N.R. Howard, C. Schulte. Computer Science Education: Perspectives on Teaching and Learning in School. Bloomsbury Academic. 2018 (1st ed.); 2023 (2nd ed.). *Semplice, lievemente più teorico*
